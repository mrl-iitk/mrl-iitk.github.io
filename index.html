Mobile Robotics Laboratory

We build intelligent machines that expand the horizon of robotic mobility. Primarily focusing on legged robots, we work on control algorithms for locomotion and manipulation, actuation systems and creative designs for agility and reliability. We look for ideas from the natural world that can help us in enhancing the ability and autonomy of these robots and make them suitable for a wide range of real-life applications.
